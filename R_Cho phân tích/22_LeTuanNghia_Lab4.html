Cu 3. Thay i binwidth ca histogram vi tham s binwidth. Th cc binwidth t 1000 n 10000 v li histogram theo c php geom_histogram(binwidth = xxx) v chn binwidth bn cho l ph hp nht. Gii thch s la chn ca bn.

  geom_histogram(mapping = aes(x = Median), binwidth = bw)
